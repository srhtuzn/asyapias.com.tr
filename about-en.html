Our History

A Secure Future Designed with Precision

Since the day it was founded, AS Yapı has earned a respectable place in the construction sector with its commitment to engineering ethics, innovative designs, and uncompromising quality standards.

We build more than just concrete structures; we create smart ecosystems where people can live safely, socialize, and respect the environment. In our projects, we use the most advanced technologies and first-class imported materials at every stage, from static calculations to landscaping details.

With the awareness that Turkey lies in an active earthquake zone, we keep static resistance at the highest level, starting from soil analysis to raft foundation systems and reinforced steel frame structures, even beyond the requirements of earthquake regulations.

2011

Foundation & First Steps

The AS Yapı brand was founded in Ankara, and the construction of the first boutique residential projects began.

2016

Large-Scale Residence Projects

Transitioned into the multi-story structure segment, delivering our first major mixed-use residence and lifestyle project.

2021

Green Buildings & LEED Certifications

Sustainability was officially registered by winning eco-friendly certifications (LEED Gold) for developed commercial plaza projects.

2026

Smart Cities & Intelligent Buildings

We continue to shape the future with automated smart cladding systems and next-generation buildings that generate their own electricity.
